In this Website Vehicles which are available for the auction are displayed .If the client clicks on the image of the vehicle all the features of the vehicle, price of the vehicle, manufactured year of the vehicle, condition, minimum amount for making a bid and when will the bidding ends and status of the bidding results whether he won the bid or not.

Existing system:
In existing Vehicle Auction system all the procedures are done manually .Documents for bidding should be submitted physically Cost for maintaing staff and area for auction is huge..It takes more effort and physical space to keep track of paper documents, to find information and to keep details secure Physical appearance of the clients are Mandatory. Less Transparency is involved. More time is required.It is Expensive to maintain Manual auction.Location of the Auction may be far away from the clients location then he cannot participate in auction.Client should attend the Auction at specified time which is not adjustable for client.Inconsistency in data entry, room for errors, miskeying information.

Proposed System:
Manual Auction system is changed into Digital Auction system. Where Digitalization and Automation is the key concepts Advantages of Proposed System: In this system less staff is needed which decreases the cost of maintainance .Physical appearance is not required , where client can manage all the things by visiting the portal .This system increases the transparency and because of automation, speed of the procedure/auction will increase. Client can join the auction from any place across the globe .Time for maintaing action is decreased. Data will be stored and maintained safely. Here the client can get good customer support and can resolve the issues in less time .which improves the Trust on the Auction. Here the clients can easily access the information of vehicles without visiting the vehicle.

ABOUT US

" An Auction Portal also knows as an online auction website or platform, is a virtual marketplace that facilitates the buying and selling of vehicles through an auction format. Unlike traditional auctions take place in physical locations, auction portals enable users to participate in auctions from the comfort of their homes or offices using their computers or mobile devices. Auctions are performed on several vehicles presented in the site. Primarily, the Starting bid is displayed on each and every vehicle. Later on,users can make more bids on the followed Vehicles "
