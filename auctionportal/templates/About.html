About?

An online auction is an auction held over the internet and accessed by internet connected devices. Similar to in-person auctions, online auctions come in a variety of types, with different bidding and selling rules. Main Aim of the project is to build a website which solves general Auction problems. Here we make the Auction into fully digital mode .Here we will deal with vehicle auctions. In this website all the vehicles which are available for the auction are displayed .If the client clicks on the image of the vehicle all the features of the vehicle, price of the vehicle, manufactured year of the vehicle, condition, minimum amount for making a bid and when will the bidding ends and status of the bidding results whether he won the bid or not. If a client want to purchase a vehicle he can visit the website and should create account/profile with basic information and then he can select the type of vehicle needed and make a bid .If the client wins the certain vehicle auction then he can purchase the vehicle .For making a bid client should pay certain amount with reference to the price of the vehicle. Here the client saves a lot of time, where generally in normal Auction it takes lot of time and physical appearcence is needed. Here we do not need the physical appearcence of the client for bidding, he should only give basic personal information .All the payments regarding the Auction are made in online mode, which improves the transparency and good customer support will be provided.
